David's Game

Rules of David's Game

  1. Each player gets 5 cards
  2. A player can put down a minimum of 2 of a kind
  3. A player can put down a run consisting of a min of 3 in a row (has to be of the same suit and colour)
  4. A player can call when they hold 5 points or less, everyone then places their cards on the table and counts their points.
  5. If a player has a lower total points than that of the caller the caller is penalised with 20 points added to their score called.
